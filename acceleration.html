3-month Accelerating Program

ICONIZ offers a three-month mentorship-driven acceleration program. Our unique ecosystem is facilitated by our world-class mentor pool consisting of seasoned entrepreneurs, corporate managers, and blockchain veterans. The program provides mentorship on product development, business development, customer acquisition, talent recruitment, and fund raising. In addition to first-class acceleration program, ICONIZ also provides guidance on token issuance, and helps startups to better communicate with their first adopters and investors to build world-class blockchain networks.
